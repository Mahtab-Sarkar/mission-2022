My Mission in 2022

Mission

A personal mission statement can help you identify your values and goals, defining what matters most to you professionally. It makes decision-making easier as well, as it clarifies whether the decisions you're making are in alignment with what you want for your life.

Vision

As the first step to developing your vision, it may be worth writing down how success will look. Define as many aspects of it as possible for you, but also identify the one most important aspect (money, fame, family etc).

Goals

Identifying areas for personal growth and defining a clear plan to hone them can have a significant effect on your career. These individual development goals may help you to learn new things, strengthen your abilities or become more effective in your role.
